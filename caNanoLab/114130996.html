NIST-NCL Joint Assay Protocol, PCC-8: Determination of Gold in Rat Tissue with Inductively Coupled Plasma Mass Spectrometry

A new class of pharmaceutical candidate based on gold nanoparticles has entered the small animal phase of clinical study. The content of gold in the blood and tissue of study animals must be determined. This technical procedure defines the analytical protocols for the determination of gold in the tissues of rats used in a clinical study.
