NCL Method GTA-6: HepG2 Hepatocarcinoma Apoptosis Assay

This protocol describes the monitoring of nanoparticle treated human hepatocarcinoma cells (HepG2) for apoptosis, as part of the in vitro NCL preclinical characterization cascade. The protocol utilizes a fluorescent method to determine the degree of caspase-3 activation.
