NCL Method ITA-6.1: Leukocyte Proliferation Assay (Immunostimulation and Immunosuppression)

This document describes a protocol for assessing the effect of a nanoparticle formulation on the basic immunologic function of human lymphocytes, i.e. measurement of lymphocyte proliferative responses. Lymphocytes are isolated from human blood anti-coagulated with Li-heparin using Ficoll-Paque Premium solution. The isolated cells are incubated with or without phytohemaglutinin (PHA-M) in the presence or absence of nanoparticles and analyzed spectrophotometrically via the MTT (3-(4, 5-dimethyl-2-thiazolyl)-2,5-diphenyl-2H-tetrazolium bromide) assay.
