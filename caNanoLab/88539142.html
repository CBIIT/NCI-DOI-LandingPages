Description: This document describes a protocol for quantitative colorimetric determination of total hemoglobin in whole blood (TBH) and plasma free hemoglobin (PFH). An increase in the plasma free hemoglobin is indicative of erythrocyte damage by the test material (a positive control substance or a nanoparticle).
