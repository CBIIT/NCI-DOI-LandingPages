NCL Method ITA-8.1: Chemotaxis Assay

This document provides a protocol for rapid, quantitative measure of the chemoattractant capacity of a nanoparticulate material.This assay represents an in vitro model, in which promyelocytic leukemia cells (HL-60) are separated from control chemoattractant or test-nanoparticles by a 3 um filter; the cell migration through the filter is then monitored by detection of cells migrated through the filter into the bottom chamber of the culture plate using the fluorescent dye Calcein AM.
