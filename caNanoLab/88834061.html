NCL Method ITA-9.1: Phagocytosis Assay

This document describes a protocol for evaluation of nanoparticle internalization by phagocytic cells, where the foreign substances are isolated via a cell membrane enclosure and degraded by the phagolysosome. This assay utilizes a luminescent-based approach in which nanoparticles are incubated with HL 60 promyelocytic cells, and the phagocytic activity is visualized with luminol. Luminol is a dye which is not luminescent unless exposed to the low pH of the phagolysosome.
