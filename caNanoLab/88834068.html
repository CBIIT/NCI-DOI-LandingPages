Description: IL-1b is a pro-inflammatory cytokine produced by various types of cells in response to inflammatory stimulus, e.g. bacterial lipopolysaccharide LPS. This document describes experimental procedure for analysis of culture supernatants by ELISA to detect presence of IL-1b. NCL protocol ITA-10 should be referred to for details of preparation of culture supernatants.
