Description: CXCR8/IL-8 is a proinflammatory chemokine produced by various types of cells in response to inflammatory stimulus, e.g. bacterial lipopolysaccharide LPS. This document describes experimental procedure for analysis of cell culture supernatants for the presence of IL-8 by enzyme linked immunosorbent assay (ELISA).
